Please vote for us!

It's the last couple of days of a competition, run by eBay and Paypal to find their members' favourite charity - and Freegle could win much needed funding!

If you have an eBay account, all you need to do is:

  1. Sign in at http://www.ebay.co.uk first (otherwise something weird happens).
  2. Go to http://www.ebay.co.uk/egw/ebay-for-charity/charity-profile/Freegle/74430 - just click here.
  3. Click on the "Save as favourite" button on the right hand side of the screen.

You don't have to make a donation, or buy, or sell. Just click a button and make Freegle your favourite charity. Freegle could win as much as £7000, which would help enormously with our running costs.

Please vote, because every vote will raise Freegle's public profile. Also, you will be entered into a draw to win a £250 prize! If you've done this already - thanks, don't do it again or you might cancel your vote!

Remember: Freegle is free to use, but not free to run. We survive on donations and opportunities like this, and manage to provide a huge service on a tiny budget. This money would really help us.
